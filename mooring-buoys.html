THE TITAN NEW FOR 2020

THE TITAN has many of the same attributes as the original “HULL FRIENDLY ” mooring buoy such as line and mast buoy storage. The PVC fender is manufactured to be non-marking. The shell of the TITAN is composed of polyethylene while the foam is identical to other “HULL FRIENDLY ” mooring buoys and will not absorb any water. You will find the TITAN a welcomed addition to IMS line of moorimg buoys.

As of January 1, 2020 Island Mooring Supplies is limiting product of its Homan “Hull Friendly’ rope nest mooring buoys to the TITAN Series. After many years of manufacturing various styles with a corresponding mariad of materials we feel the TITAN offers the very best combination of quality, innovation and value to our customers. The TITAN exterior shell is composed of polyethylene while the flotation is provided by a closed cell foam that will not absorb water. With a non marring PVCfender your buoy will not damage your vessels hull. The buoy design provides a larger area for pendant storage and fittings while allowing for mast buoy storage. The Homan “Hull Friendly” TITAN mooring buoy is the evolution of many years of manufacturing mooring buoys..
